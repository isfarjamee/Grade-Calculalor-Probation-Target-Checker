🎓 BRACU CGPA Calculator

CGPA Calculator · Probation Checker · Target Planner

📖 Quick Guide

🎯 CGPA Calculator

Find out exactly where your CGPA lands after this semester.

Step 1 — Enter your current CGPA and total credits completed.
Step 2 — If you're retaking any courses, enter how many. For each retake, provide its credit hours and the grade you previously earned — the calculator adjusts everything automatically.
Step 3 — Enter the number of new courses you're taking this semester, hit Generate, then fill in each course's credit and your expected GPA.
Step 4 — Hit Calculate and your updated cumulative CGPA appears instantly.

⚠️ Probation Checker

Find the minimum grade you need this semester to stay clear of academic probation. At BRACU, a CGPA below 2.0 puts you on probation.

Step 1 — Enter your current CGPA, completed credits, and any retake course details.
Step 2 — Generate your semester courses. Enter grades you've already received — leave the rest blank.
Step 3 — Hit Check. If grades are pending, you'll see exactly what GPA you need in each remaining course to stay safe. If all grades are in, it tells you whether you're safe or on probation — no guesswork.

🚀 Target Checker

Set a CGPA goal and find out whether it's within reach — and exactly what it takes to get there.

Step 1 — Enter your current CGPA and credits completed.
Step 2 — Enter your remaining credits (any number — the calculator handles odd credits automatically).
Step 3 — Set your target CGPA.
Step 4 — Hit Check. You'll see a realistic grade distribution plan — how many courses need which grade to hit your target, starting from what's actually achievable.
